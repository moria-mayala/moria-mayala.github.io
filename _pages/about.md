I am temporary lecturer and research assistant (ATER) in the Department of Mathematics at Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne.
I recently completed my PhD in statistics learning in  the Statistics departement of at [LPSM](https://www.lpsm.paris/) (Sorbonne Université). I  also had  the opportunity  to visit and to work with two other research groups: [LMV](https://lmv.math.cnrs.fr/) (UVSQ, Yvelines) and [WPI](https://www.wpi.ac.at/) (Universität Wien). My research activities  focus on theoretical statistics and Machine Learning,  with a particular emphasis on  an algorithm of Machine Learning called random forests. \
I was supervised by [Olivier Wintenberger](http://wintenberger.fr/) (LPSM) and [Charles Tillier](http://tillier.perso.math.cnrs.fr/) (LMV).
